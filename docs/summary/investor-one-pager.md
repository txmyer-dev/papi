# The People's AI (PAPI) – Investor One‑Page Summary
**Date:** 2026‑06‑05

## Mission
A **Public Benefit Corp** delivering a free‑inference bridging layer that unlocks ~1.7 B free AI tokens/month for developers, researchers, and NGOs. Our revenue comes from premium managed instances, enterprise SLAs, analytics add‑ons and professional services.

## Problem
- **AI cost barrier:** $20‑$200+/mo for proprietary LLM APIs.
- **Fragmented free tiers:** 16+ providers, each isolated, rate‑limited, and difficult to manage.
- **Wasted capacity:** 1.7 B tokens/month sit idle.

## Solution
- **Single OpenAI‑compatible endpoint** (`/v1/chat/completions`).
- **Intelligent routing & fallback** across all free providers.
- **Encrypted key storage**; optional user‑provided keys for higher limits.
- **Open‑source core** (`FreeLLMAPI`).
